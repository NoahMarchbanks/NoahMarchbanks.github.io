Audio Design

Radio Ad Spot: For this project, I was asked to record myself reading a script and then add the appropriate designs to the audio to convey the tone and message of the script. I added and edited sounds such as music samples and special FX.

Radio Spot

Upon completion of this project, I understood the concepts that are used in audio design and a lot more goes on behind the scenes than one might think. I learned that sounds must be given the correct pitch and effect to match the tone of the purpose. For example, if the purpose is to portray the importance of giving back to others, you might add some slow, somber music to create an empathetic tone.

Graphic Design

Logo Design: For this project, I was asked to create and design logos for a fictional company by the name of “RetroWeird Software”. I was given the branding of the company and had to create logos that portrayed the brand in a professional way that is unique and pertains to the theme using color theory.

Upon completion of this project, I was capable of applying branding and styling techniques that compliment the focus of the brand and the purpose of the company.



Thumbnail Design: For this project, I was asked to create a thumnail for a documentary about the Joplin tornado story. I was given images to choose from and edit together to create a scene that follows the theme and matches the tone of the documentary.

Upon completion of this project, I was able to understand how to create a scene that accurately depicts the tone of a given video and the importance of minute details.
