Noah Marchbanks

My name is Noah Marchbanks and I am currently a freshman majoring in Information Technology at the University of Missouri. I am from a small town at the very bottom of Missouri and formed an interest in technology when I was young. I love to code and have been doing it in some form for the majority of my life.

I am very creative and love to challenge myself by jumping into different coding languages. I also like to venture into graphic and audio design and practice designing different things that will help me bring my programs to life.
I love superheros and love to learn about them. I am a huge fan of both Marvel and DC. They have always fascinated me and I love to imagine the different fights that could ensue between any two different heroes (or villains).
I also love video games and usually play a variety during my free time. As I have started to learn and practice coding and designing, I have developed a different way of looking at video games. I have grown an incredible appreciation for the process that came before the final product. That meaning, whenever I'm playing the game, I try to envision the code and designing techniques that were used to create what I am seeing before me. I hope to do the same one day and to be able to create something that people all over the world can enjoy.
